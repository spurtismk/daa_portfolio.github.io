Restaurant Management System

System Overview

The Restaurant Management System is designed to streamline various operations in a restaurant. The system handles multiple tasks, including customer queue management, table reservations, inventory management, menu and billing, delivery tracking, and lodging for customers. By integrating all these features into a single platform, the system ensures smooth operations and enhances the customer experience. Below is a brief explanation of each core functionality:

Customer Queue Management

The Customer Queue Management system ensures that customers are served in the order they arrive. It follows a First In, First Out (FIFO) approach, meaning that the first customer to join the queue will be the first one to be served. This helps in maintaining an orderly flow of customers, reducing frustration, and optimizing the time spent on serving customers, ensuring no one is skipped or left waiting for too long.

Table Reservation System

The Table Reservation System allows customers to book tables in advance, ensuring that there are no issues with table availability. It manages the status of each table, tracking which ones are reserved and which are free. This eliminates the risk of double bookings and ensures that customers get the table they reserved, improving overall service efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Inventory Management

The Inventory Management system tracks the availability of food ingredients, beverages, and other supplies required to run the restaurant. This system helps in monitoring stock levels and ensures that items are replenished on time. It alerts the staff when certain items are running low, preventing disruptions in service and ensuring that popular menu items are always available for customers.

Menu Management and Billing

The Menu Management and Billing system handles the listing of menu items, their prices, and customer orders. Staff can update the menu with new dishes or changes in pricing, keeping the menu dynamic. When customers place their orders, the system calculates the total cost, applies taxes or discounts, and generates a bill for the customer. This system ensures that billing is accurate, and the menu is always up-to-date.

Delivery Management

The Delivery Management system tracks customer orders that are placed for delivery. It ensures that each delivery is processed efficiently, from order confirmation to dispatch and final delivery. The system tracks the status of each order, ensuring that deliveries are made on time and that customers are kept informed throughout the process.

Lodging System

The Lodging System manages the booking of rooms for guests who wish to stay at the restaurant’s associated lodging facilities. It tracks room availability, guest information, and booking dates. This system helps in managing room reservations, ensuring there are no overbookings, and providing a seamless check-in and check-out experience for guests.

Menu Management and Inventory Management

I developed the Menu Management and Inventory Management functionalities for the system. These two aspects play a vital role in maintaining the flow of operations in the restaurant, and I focused on creating efficient, reliable features for both.

Menu Management

The Menu Management system allows restaurant staff to easily manage the food and drink items listed on the menu. This feature enables the addition, removal, and modification of menu items. Staff can update the availability of specific dishes based on the current stock, ensuring that customers are only shown items that are available. Additionally, prices can be updated quickly to reflect changes in ingredient costs or promotions. The system integrates with the billing system, so when an item is ordered, it automatically reflects in the customer's bill. The Menu Management system is a powerful tool that helps keep the restaurant’s offerings organized and customer-friendly, making it easier for both staff and customers to navigate the dining experience.

Inventory Management

The Inventory Management system tracks the ingredients and supplies needed for the kitchen to prepare dishes. It allows the restaurant to monitor stock levels in real-time and alerts staff when stock is running low. This helps in maintaining the right balance of inventory, preventing waste and shortages. The system automatically updates the inventory as orders are placed and ingredients are used, providing accurate and up-to-date information on stock levels. Additionally, the system generates reports on inventory usage, helping the restaurant plan for future orders and avoid overstocking or understocking of ingredients. Efficient inventory management is essential for a restaurant to minimize waste, reduce costs, and ensure that customers receive their orders without delay.
